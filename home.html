Frequently asked questions
— How can I reset my password?
You can change your password at anytime! If you are logged in, you can change your password by going to Account & Settings > Update Password.
If you forgot your password, simply go to your login page, click on "Forgot Your Password?". Instructions to reset your password will be sent to your email address.
— Where can I get support?
If you have questions you can contact us through email at Drivera Support. You can also click on the "help" icon at the bottom left corner of your screen. We are always here to answer any questions!
— Can I schedule single and group student lessons?
Drivera is adjustable to all your needs. You can schedule lessons with a single student, a group of students or no students at all! It all depends on what your business needs are.
— Can I modify the calendar to my preferences?
Absolutely! The calendar is an essential tool to manage your business efficiently. You need to be able to find the information you need right away, therefore, the calendar has adjustable settings to match your preferred view, color coding, filters and more.
— What reports and analytics are available within drive your school?

Drivera offers several reports and analytics to help manage your driving school efficiently. Some of the available reports include student progress reports, financial reports, and more. These reports can be customized to display the information you need to make informed decisions about your business.
