Student management

Our software allows driving schools to track student progress, record test results, and monitor attendance. This functionality helps driving schools ensure that their students are getting the support and guidance they need to succeed.

Scheduling 

Our driving school management system includes powerful scheduling tools that allow driving schools to easily manage lesson schedules, instructor availability, and student preferences. 

Analytics

Our software provides detailed analytics and reporting on a range of key metrics, such as student progress, instructor performance, and revenue. This functionality helps driving schools make data-driven decisions, identify areas for improvement, and optimize their operations.

About

Welcome to our Drivera management system!

Our driving school management system was developed with the goal of making the administration of driving schools as simple and intuitive as possible. We know that managing a driving school can be a complex and time-consuming task, and our software is designed to take some of that burden off your shoulders. 

We're passionate about using technology to make people's lives easier, and we believe that our driving school management system can do just that. Whether you're a small, independent driving school or a large chain of schools, our software can help you streamline your operations and provide a better experience for your students.
